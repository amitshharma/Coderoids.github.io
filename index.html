About Us

The Coderoids is a Business Process Outsourcing Company primarily into five different businesses vertical which are Fixed Asset Management, Inventory Management, Manpower Outsourcing, Information Technology and Process Audit & Consultancy. We are centrally located state of the art back end processing facilities in Gurgaon.

The Coderoids offers a wide range of specialized, multidisciplinary professional services that meet the immediate as well as long term needs of any business. The Coderoids collaborate with each client to deliver customized business process outsourcing solutions that range in complexity from individual transaction-based functions to highly scale up operations.
